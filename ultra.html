Summary

"ULTRAKILL" is a fast paced, sandbox area-shooter. You play as a robot called V1 who in a desperate attempt to survive, has jumped into hell to feed off of the blood of demons. The game provides many secrets and weapons you can choose from (18 in total) that allow you to pull of mutliple tricks to maximise your damage. It was developed by Arsi "Hakita" Patala and is currently in early access.

More Detail

ULTRAKILL's gameplay combines features from classic shooters like Quake, modern shooters like Doom (2016) and character action games like Devil May Cry. The game places emphasis on movement, dodging attacks, and efficient gunplay in its arena-style levels. In addition, the player's health can only be restored by showering in the blood of enemies, requiring the player to adopt an aggressive playstyle that regularly places them in close combat with their opponents. The game's prologue and first act were released in early access on September 3, 2020, and a free demo is available on Steam.
