Summary

"My Summer Car" is a unique simulation game developed by Amistech Games. Set in rural Finland during the 1990s, the game plunges players into the shoes of a teenager tasked with building, maintaining, and upgrading their own car while navigating the challenges of everyday life in the Finnish countryside. The game offers a highly detailed and immersive experience, where players must assemble their car from scratch, piece by piece, while ensuring they have enough money for fuel, food, and other necessities. Players are responsible for tuning and maintaining their car, including tasks like adjusting the engine, fixing mechanical issues, and passing vehicle inspections. Beyond car mechanics, players must also manage their character's needs, such as hunger, thirst, and fatigue. Exploration of the open world is encouraged, with various activities available including fishing, drinking with friends at the local pub, and participating in amateur rally races. Despite its seemingly mundane premise, "My Summer Car" offers a surprisingly deep and engaging gameplay experience, blending simulation, survival, and open-world elements into a humorous and immersive adventure set in the idyllic Finnish countryside.
